FAQs

What is Quiz Rizz?

Quiz Rizz is an interactive online platform where users can solve quizzes on a wide range of topics, from general knowledge and current events to specialized subjects. It offers an engaging way to test your knowledge, improve your skills, and have fun while learning. Perfect for both casual learners and quiz enthusiasts, Quiz Rizz makes learning entertaining with its user-friendly interface and diverse quiz categories.

How can I start a quiz?

Simply navigate to the subject whose quiz you want to create, choose the difficulty, and you are good to go with your quiz.

What features are included in the Premium Plan?

The Premium Plan offers AI support, access to leaderboards, mentor support, and exclusive 3D video content to enhance your learning journey.

Is there a free version available?

Yes, we offer a basic free version that allows limited quiz creations and access to our study materials. Upgrade to Premium for more features.
